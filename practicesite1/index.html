About Me

I am majoring in Arts and Visual Technologies at George Mason University with a concentration in Studio Arts and a minor in Dynamic Publishing. In my free time I love to listen to music, create, play video games, and watch different shows and movies. For my future career, I hope to work in publishing, continuing my journey with illustration and artist books.
