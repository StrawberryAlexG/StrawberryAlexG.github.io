About Me
Hello, my name is Alejandra (Alex) Giron (she/her), I am 22 years old, I am from Virginia, and I have one sister. In my free time I love to listen to music, create, play video games, and watch different shows and movies. I really enjoy sketching and planning future projects, centered around arts and crafts. I have recently been reading poetry books and comics, which I have been really enjoying, more specifically I have found illustrated poetry books, which have inspired me greatly. For my future career, I hope to work in publishing, continuing my journey with illustration, comics, publishing, and artist books.

Education & Experiance
Alex Giron studied at Northern Virginia Community College in 2020 where she earned her AFA in fine arts. Alex then transferred to George Mason University to pursue a BFA in Arts and Visual Technologies in 2022 with a concentration in Studio Art. She is currently studying to earn her BFA and studying for a minor in Dynamic Publishing.
Currently working in an internship with Stillhouse Press (Summer 2024)
Artwork: Mediums and Themes
With my artwork I like to work themes such as: nature, surrealism, mythology, and trauma. I like to work with themes I am passionate about and can go deep with the research. I am a huge fan of surrealism and work with it a lot in my artwork. For my senior project I have combined surrealism with personal experience in my life and want to continue to explore that combination of themes in future projects. I especially enjoy comics, illustration and book arts. I am continuously trying to find ways to push these mediums and shape them beyond their limits in art. In my practice, I continue to explore more art mediums and themes, exploring new mediums and putting them in my artwork, is something I plan to keep doing to grow my practice in art for the future.
Mediums and art forms I enjoy working with include: book arts, illustration, paper craft, and painting. For illustration I enjoy working with inking pens, colored pencils, and markers. My experience with painting includes watercolor and acrylics. My favorite material to work with is paper, because of its versatility and it is also a material I use the most when it comes to my artwork and practice. I have experience with Cricut cutting machines and materials, as well as digital art through a tablet. I also experiment with materials, mixing mediums, which involves sewing/stitching, fabrics, jewelry making, and beading.
