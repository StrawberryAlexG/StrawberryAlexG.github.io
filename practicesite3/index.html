Alex Giron

Artist and Creator

Portfolio

My artwork consists of themes of nature, surrealism, mythology, and trauma. The process of my artwork usually consists of an idea, research, sketches, and drafts. I have explored many mediums of art and am still discovering and trying new ones. I do more studio art which consists of drawing, painting, illustration, and building. I especially enjoy comics and book arts.

About Me

Hello, my name is Alejandra (Alex) Giron, I am 22 years old, I am from Virginia, and I have one sister. I am majoring in Arts and Visual Technologies at George Mason University with a concentration in Studio Arts and a minor in Dynamic Publishing. In my free time I love to listen to music, create, play video games, and watch different shows and movies. For my future career, I hope to work in publishing, continuing my journey with illustration and artist books.
